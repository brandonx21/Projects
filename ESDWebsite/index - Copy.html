With over 30 years of experience in the control of Electrostatic Attraction and Electrostatic Discharge, Donn Bellmore has specialized in Automated Handling Equipment and other assembly processes. A broad array of ESD consulting services are available from automated and manual process audits and Production Line Charge Profiles to training and assistance in obtaining Facility Certification. The usual results of testing completed in your facility are recommendations of improved materials and their use, and improved grounding of process equipment to increase ESD Control effectiveness. Also available are audits of existing ESD Control Policies and programs to determine performance and compliance to the ANSI/ESD S20.20 Standard for Developing Electrostatic Discharge Control Program. Don't worry, if you do not have a policy or program, we can design and tailor a program for your facility that will satisfy the requirements for Facility Certification.

Advanced ESD+ Service crucial elements of your industry, and as such, your trust and satisfaction of our service is our upmost priority. Take a look at what other customers have said in our Testimonials, browse our Services, or Contact Us with any questions.
